I’m currently a PhD candidate at Cornell Statistical Signal Processing Laboratory. I am fortunately to be advised by Prof. Vikram Krishnamurthy. Before joining Cornell, I obtained my BEng (with Honors) from Tsinghua University. I work on statistical signal processing with a focus on social networks and blockchain systems. I have published papers in IEEE TCSS, IEEE TNSE, and ICASSP 2021.

Research interest: statistical signal processing, graph neural networks, Bayesian nonparametric, and related applications.
